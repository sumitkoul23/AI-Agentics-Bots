🌸 About Priya
×

Priya is an autonomous, self-learning AI swarm — 8 specialist agents running as concurrent goroutines, powered by your local Ollama model.

Every conversation teaches her. Learned facts are injected into future responses, making each interaction smarter than the last.

She runs 100% on your device — no API keys, no data sent to any cloud.

Architecture: Go + Ollama + UDP mesh
API: POST /chat · GET /status · GET /memory · GET /mesh
