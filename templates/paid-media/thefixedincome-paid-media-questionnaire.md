# Let's Plan Your Paid Media — thefixedincome.in

Hi team,

Since paid ads are fairly new for you and there isn't much past data to lean on yet, we've kept this short. Just the essentials we need to launch sensibly and start learning fast. Answer what you can — anything unknown, write *"not sure yet"* and we'll figure it out together.

---

## 1. The basics

- Which product should we push first — bonds, T-bills, FDs, debt MFs?
- Who's your ideal customer? (a quick sketch is enough — e.g. "salaried, 30–45, metro")
- What's the one action that matters most: a lead, a completed CKYC, or a first investment?
- Why should someone choose you over a regular bank FD?

## 2. Goal & budget

- Primary goal of this campaign — leads, CKYCs, or first investments?
- Monthly budget you're comfortable starting with?
- Any go-live date or seasonality to ride (FY-end, a bond issue, tax season)?

> Since past data is limited, we'll start with sensible targets and refine them once real numbers come in — no need to guess benchmarks now.

## 3. Who we're targeting

- Age range, and any cities/states to focus on (or avoid)?
- Languages for targeting and ads — English, Hindi, regional?
- Interests or professions worth prioritising (mutual funds, stock market, salaried, business owners)?
- Any customer/CRM list we can use to target or exclude people, or to find lookalikes?
- Anyone we must leave out — existing customers, ineligible/under-18, NRIs if not allowed?
